About

Last updated: March 31, 2022

I created this site because I despise any and all infringment on the basic human rights to privacy and voluntary exchange. Note that this is an early version of the site, I aim to make it drastically more comprehensive and visually-appealing in the future.

This project was heavily inspired by kycnot.me and privacytools.io, both of which are incredibly helpful resources.



Q: Why should I trust you, or the accuracy of your recommendations?
You shouldn't trust me, or anyone else making software recommendations for that matter. Do your own research. I am providing mere suggestions for what may be helpful, it is ultimately up to you to figure out what works best in your case.

Q: What metrics do you use to assign your ratings? And why isn't there any in-depth explanation or discussion of each rating?
The ratings are somewhat arbitrary (at least for now), but I generally consider the following: UX, decentralization, quality of the product, accessibility, privacy, dev/code transparency, reputation, track record, and others. And for now I figured that it would be best to keep the ratings brief, because they'd quickly become unwieldy on this simple HTML page. Though I do plan to be more comprehensive once I get more organized.

Q: Why does this site look so ugly? This is worse webdesign than what I saw in the '90s.
This is an early prototype, and I have very limited expirience with webdesign. My goal is to gradually upgrade it into something more appealing, but for now you'll have to deal with the simplistic design. At least it fits in with 90% of the other websites in onionland, so I can use that as an excuse.

Q: Is the code for this site open-source?
Yes. Here is a link to the github page. Alternatively, you can right-click and select "View Page Source"; for now this site is 1 file of pure HTML, so that page will show you everything. Also, please don't harass me for my terrible formatting.

Q: Is there a clearnet (non-onion) version of this site?
No, but I've posted a "mirror" on github.

Q: Can I donate to this project?
